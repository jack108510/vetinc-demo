What is Vet INC?

Vet INC is a revenue maximization platform for veterinary clinics. We analyze every price in your practice, find what's underpriced, and make small changes that add up to significant revenue — without losing clients.

How it works:

1. Upload your practice data (AVImark, Cornerstone, ezyVet)

2. We analyze every service against inflation, market rates, and demand elasticity

3. You approve the changes you want

4. We track results and continue optimizing
